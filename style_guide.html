My Ethos

About Me

I'm Lavanya, a third-year Interactive Arts and Technology student at SFU with a love for UI/UX and game design. I believe good design is felt before it's understood — clean, considered, and always purposeful. I'm constantly exploring new tools, techniques, and creative territories, because curiosity is what keeps my work honest. Beyond screens, I pour that same energy into hobbies and art that keep me inspired. Scroll through to catch a glimpse. :D

Ethos keywords:

Clear

Every element earns its place. Nothing is decorative for its own sake.

Minimal

Breathing room is a design decision, not an absence of content.

Organised

Consistent structure guides the eye — hierarchy over decoration.

Intentional

Deliberate choices in spacing, type, and tone. Nothing accidental.

Soft-spoken

The work speaks first. The presentation doesn't compete.

Cohesive

Everything belongs to the same visual language — nothing out of place.
